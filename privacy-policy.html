Who we are

Planscape is a day-planning app for iOS and Android, provided by Antonio Gutierrez Sanchez, trading as Planscape, operating as a sole trader based in the United Kingdom.

We have not appointed a Data Protection Officer, as this is not required under UK GDPR Article 37 for a business of our size and processing activities.

For any privacy questions or requests, contact us at support@planscapeai.com.

Legal basis for processing

Under UK GDPR, we must have a valid legal basis for each way we use your data. The basis varies depending on the purpose:

Purpose Legal basis
Creating and operating your account, generating and storing plans, processing subscriptions Contract. Necessary to provide the service you've signed up for.
Security monitoring, fraud and abuse prevention, authentication logs Legitimate interests. Necessary to keep the service secure, balanced against your right to privacy.
Aggregated, non-identifying analysis to improve reliability and features Legitimate interests. We don't use this to profile individual users.
Marketing communications Consent. We do not currently send marketing emails. If this changes, we'll ask for your opt-in first, and you can withdraw consent at any time.

AI and your data

Generating a plan sends a request to OpenAI's API. Specifically, we send: your selected mood, social context, time block, budget, and city, your stored preferences (likes/dislikes), and a short list of your recent plan titles for the same city (to avoid repetitive suggestions). We do not send your email address, name, or any direct identifier to OpenAI.

Per OpenAI's published policy, data sent through their API is not used to train their models by default, and is retained only for abuse and safety monitoring for up to 30 days before deletion.

All inputs to plan generation are selected from fixed options (mood, budget, time block, and similar enumerated choices) rather than free text, which limits the chance of accidentally sharing sensitive personal information through this feature.

The itinerary text and place suggestions returned by OpenAI are combined with real venue data from Google Places, and the result is what's shown to you as a draft plan.

Storage and security

Your data is stored on Supabase's infrastructure, hosted on Amazon Web Services. All data in transit is encrypted using TLS. Access to your data is restricted by row-level security policies, so your data is only accessible to you and to the service functions that need it to operate the app.

While we take reasonable technical precautions, no system is completely secure. If you become aware of a security issue, contact us at support@planscapeai.com.

International data transfers

Supabase (via AWS), OpenAI, RevenueCat, Apple, and Google are based in or operate infrastructure in the United States. Where your data is transferred outside the UK, we rely on one of the following safeguards required by UK GDPR:

The UK extension to the EU-US Data Privacy Framework (the "UK-US Data Bridge"), where the recipient organisation is certified under it; or

The UK International Data Transfer Agreement (IDTA) or the UK Addendum to the EU Standard Contractual Clauses, where certification doesn't apply.

You can request details of the specific safeguard used for a given transfer, or a copy of the relevant agreement, by contacting support@planscapeai.com.

Data retention

We keep different categories of data for different lengths of time:

Category Retention
Account, profile, plans, folders, preferences Retained while your account is active. Deleted from our primary database immediately when you delete your account in-app. Backups are retained for up to 30 days as part of routine rotation before being permanently overwritten.
Purchase and subscription records Retained by Apple and RevenueCat according to their own policies, typically for several years to meet financial and tax record-keeping obligations.
Transactional email logs (Mailtrap) Retained by Mailtrap for a limited period for delivery troubleshooting, per their published retention policy.
AI processing logs (OpenAI) Retained by OpenAI for up to 30 days for abuse and safety monitoring, then deleted. Not used for model training by default.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data. Some rights apply differently depending on the legal basis for a given use of your data — for example, data we must keep for legal obligations can't be erased on request until that obligation ends.

  • Access Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure Delete your account and all associated data directly in the app (Profile → Delete Account), or contact us to request deletion.
  • Portability Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Restriction Ask us to restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Objection Object to processing carried out under legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent Where we rely on consent (e.g. future marketing emails), withdraw it at any time without affecting earlier processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@planscapeai.com. We will respond within one month.

We do not carry out any processing that produces legal or similarly significant effects based solely on automated decision-making.

If you are in the UK, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

Children's privacy

Planscape is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

In the UK, the ICO's Age Appropriate Design Code can apply to online services that are likely to be accessed by under-18s, even where the service isn't specifically aimed at children. Planscape does not currently verify a user's age at sign-up. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

California residents

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (as amended) may give you additional rights. We do not sell or share your personal information for cross-context behavioural advertising. The categories of data we collect, their sources, and the purposes for collecting them are described in the "Data we collect" section above.

You can request to know, delete, or correct your personal information by contacting support@planscapeai.com. We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.
