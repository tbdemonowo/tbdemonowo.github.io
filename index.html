How Planscape works

What is Planscape?

Planscape is an AI-powered day-planning app for iOS and Android. You tell it your mood, who you're with, your budget, how much time you have, and your location — and it generates a personalised 3-stop itinerary using real, nearby places.

Plans are yours to save, schedule to a date, share with friends, and organise into folders.

How do I create a plan?
  1. 1Tap the + button on the home screen.
  2. 2Pick your mood, context, time block, budget, and location.
  3. 3Tap Generate Plan. Your itinerary is ready in seconds.
  4. 4Preview the plan, then tap Save Plan to keep it — or discard and try again.
Can I refresh a single stop in my plan?

Yes — open a saved plan and tap the refresh icon on any step. Planscape will suggest a different place for that stop without touching the rest of your itinerary.

Step refresh is a Pro feature. Changes are shown as a preview and only saved when you tap Save Changes.

Why does Planscape need my location?

Location is used to find real places near you when generating a plan. You can also type any city manually — location permission isn't required to use the app.

Can I share plans with friends?

Sharing is free on all tiers, including Free. Open any saved plan and tap the share icon to send it via Messages, WhatsApp, or any other app.

What's included in Pro?

Pro includes 15 plans per week, step refresh on saved plans, calendar view, plan scheduling, and unlimited folders. It's available as a monthly (£4.99/mo) or yearly (£49.90/yr) subscription.

What are credits?

Credits are one-time in-app purchases that give you extra plans. They come in packs of 5 (£0.99), 15 (£2.49), and 30 (£3.99). Credits never expire and work on top of both Free and Pro allowances.

Account and subscriptions

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple or Google, not through Planscape directly. The steps depend on which device you're using.

On iOS:

  1. 1Open Settings on your device.
  2. 2Tap your name at the top, then Subscriptions.
  3. 3Tap Planscape and choose your option.

You can also manage subscriptions via the App Store > your profile icon > Subscriptions.

On Android:

  1. 1Open the Google Play Store app.
  2. 2Tap your profile icon, then Payments & subscriptions > Subscriptions.
  3. 3Tap Planscape and choose your option.
I was charged but my Pro features aren't showing

First, try closing and reopening Planscape. If that doesn't help, go to your profile in the app and tap Restore Purchases. This syncs your subscription status from Apple.

If the issue persists, contact us with your Apple ID email and we'll look into it.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile in the app and tap Delete Account. This permanently deletes all your plans, folders, and account data. It can't be undone.

If you have an active subscription, cancel it separately through Apple's Subscriptions settings before deleting your account.

I forgot my password or can't sign in

On the login screen, tap Forgot password? and enter your email. You'll receive a link to reset it.

If you signed up with Google or Sign in with Apple, use the same method you used originally — those accounts don't have a Planscape password.

Troubleshooting

My plan failed to generate

This is usually a connectivity issue. Check you have a stable internet connection and try again. If it fails repeatedly:

  1. 1Force-close Planscape and reopen it.
  2. 2Check that your location or chosen city is set correctly.
  3. 3Try again — generation failures don't use up your plan allowance.

If problems persist, contact us so we can investigate.

The app crashes or behaves unexpectedly

Make sure you're on the latest version of Planscape (check the App Store or Google Play for updates). If the issue happens on a specific screen or action, let us know what you were doing and which device model and OS version you're on — it helps us track it down quickly.
